Our Story

BUILT ON TRUST.

Forge Construction started in a small shop in Seattle in 1987. What began as a crew of four has grown into a full-service firm—but we've never lost the hands-on ethic that got us here.

We're still on the job site. We still answer the phone. And we still treat every project like it's our name on the door—because it is.
